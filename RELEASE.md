- `apps.hstream.io/v1alpha2`

    - `HStreamDB` with [rqlite](https://hub.docker.com/layers/hstreamdb/hstream/rqlite/images/sha256-97b46999e61cae9c1c810020c33e46014c930d99ccc2579a065441ce54a86c5d?context=explore)
